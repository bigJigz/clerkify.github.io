About Clerkify

We built Clerkify around a straightforward idea — that every client deserves the same level of care and attention, regardless of how many files are on our desk.

Your Property, Our Priority

Clerkify is a residential real estate clerical services firm operating in Ontario. We specialize exclusively in the processing and closing of residential transactions — purchases, sales, refinances, and title transfers — all performed under the supervision of a licensed Ontario real estate lawyer. We do not do a little bit of everything. We do this, and we do it well. That singular focus means our team knows every stage of a residential closing inside and out, and our clients feel that difference from the first conversation to the final report.

Who We Are

We are a team of experienced real estate law clerks with a shared commitment to precision, communication, and client care. Our work sits at the intersection of legal process and personal service — technical enough to protect your interests, approachable enough to make you feel informed and at ease. Every file is handled by someone who has closed hundreds of transactions and treats yours with the same attention they would give their own.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Exclusive focus on residential real estate — no divided attention
  • Consistent, proactive communication at every stage of your file
  • Flexible scheduling including evenings, weekends, and mobile signings
  • One included mobile signing anywhere within the GTA
  • All work supervised by a licensed Ontario real estate lawyer
  • Transparent pricing with no surprise fees at closing

Our Approach

Real estate transactions carry real stakes. We approach every file with the understanding that for most people, this is one of the largest financial decisions they will ever make. That means we communicate clearly, manage timelines proactively, and flag anything unusual before it becomes a problem. We do not wait for issues to surface — we look ahead so you do not have to.

Our Commitment

We measure success the same way our clients do — a transaction that closes on time, without surprises, with every document in order. That outcome does not happen by accident. It is the result of thorough preparation, careful review, and consistent follow-through on every file we take on. We are proud of our track record and work every day to protect it.

Integrity

Straightforward about what we do, what we charge, and what to expect. No fine print, no surprises.

Reliability

Your closing date is not flexible, and neither are we. We build timelines that work and follow through on them.

Communication

You will always know where your file stands. An informed client is a confident one.

Precision

The details matter in real estate law. We check, review, and verify — because getting it right the first time protects everyone.
