The Clerkify Process

1

Client Intake

Confirm parties, ID verification, collect engagement documents, and establish timelines.

2

Title & Off-Title Search

Order title, review instruments, check taxes/utilities, verify legal description, and note encumbrances.

3

Document Drafting

Prepare transfer, charge/mortgage, affidavits, statements, undertakings, and client signing package.

4

Lender & Opposing Counsel

Reconcile figures, request payouts, obtain instructions, and coordinate signing and funding logistics.

5

Closing & Registration

Release funds, e-register, confirm discharges/registrations, exchange keys and undertakings.

6

Post-Closing & Reporting

Deliver final report, receipts, and registered docs; archive the file and diarize any follow-ups.
