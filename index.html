How We Can Help

Specialized clerical support for every stage of your real estate transaction.

Purchase & Sale

Full-cycle support from agreement review to key exchange.

We manage documents, title searches, lender coordination, and registration so your purchase or sale closes without complications.

Title & Registry

Know exactly what you are buying before you sign.

We conduct thorough title and off-title searches, flag encumbrances, and ensure your investment is protected from day one.

Refinancing

Restructure your mortgage with confidence and clarity.

The Clerkify Process

1

Client Intake

We confirm all parties, complete ID verification, collect engagement documents, and establish a clear timeline from the outset.

2

Title & Off-Title Search

We order and review title, check tax and utility status, verify the legal description, and identify any encumbrances that need to be addressed.

3

Document Drafting

We prepare the full closing package including transfer, charge, affidavits, statements of adjustments, undertakings, and client signing documents.

4

Lender & Counsel Coordination

We reconcile figures, request mortgage payouts, comply with lender instructions, and coordinate signing and funding logistics with all parties.

5

Closing & Registration

We release funds, complete electronic registration via Teraview, confirm all discharges and registrations, and coordinate key exchange.

6

Post-Closing & Reporting

We deliver your final report, registered documents, and receipts — then archive the file and follow up on any outstanding undertakings.
